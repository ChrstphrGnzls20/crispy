Who is Crispy

We at Crispy envision a world where people are connected through media shared throughout the internet. Where viewers and content creators can be as close as possible without feeling worried on insecure about the other party, where hard work is supported and their commitment to the enjoyable content is protected rigorously. A place where people can gather, laugh, and enjoy content they like together with others, live and in real time, no matter how long or short the content may be. To foster connections with talented people and give them their due recognition and opportunity, and to provide everyone with quality entertainment in both the short-form and livestreaming industry.
